BMW Cat event 2023

The premium automaker is set to present its largest portfolio of vehicles since the BMW World in 2015 at the Malaysia Autoshow 2023 from 4th to 7th May. Kuala Lumpur, 1st May 2023 – BMW Group Malaysia will be presenting a range of All-New models as well as one-of-a-kind 20th Year Anniversary special deals at the upcoming Malaysia Autoshow 2023 in MAEPS. Hans de Visser, Managing Director of BMW Group Malaysia said, “As the No.1 premium automaker in Malaysia for three consecutive years, we look forward to presenting our extensive line-up of exceptional vehicles at the annual Malaysia Autoshow – one of the most highly-anticipated automotive events of the year. With rising interest in our electrified portfolio amongst customers new and old, we are thrilled to be delivering on our promise of Sheer Electric Driving Pleasure to more EV enthusiasts at the Autoshow next week, along with other powerful vehicles from across our portfolios that are sure to appeal to the individual BMW or MINI owner.”
